Pokemon route spawns

What is the purpose of this tool?

I made this just as a helper as I was completing the BDSP national dex, to keep track of the mons I encountered on each route and to later compare that against the spawn rate tables that you can find in sites such as serebii, because I was kinda suspectful about some mons not being as usual as they said they were.
Anyway, use it at your convenience and remember that the code is public in case you want to do anything more specialized.
